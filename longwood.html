Longwood Gardens

Longwood Gardens is the most iconic garden in the Brandywine Valley and has many things to see. Take a look below at some iconic highlights of what Longwood has to offer.

Flowers Of Untold Beauty

Each flower is unique and everywhere you look there is a new flower design. Each flower is uses for it's specific color and qualities in order to create one of the best gardens around.

Inspiring Events, Genius Designs

There is a new event for each season whether it is the Orchid Extravaganza, The Chrysanthemum Festival or a seasonally themed Fountain Musical Show, there is always something special at Longwood. With something new to see just about every day, there are more than enough reasons to head down to Longwood and check it out!

Legendary History, Masterfully Told

Longwood Gardens is known for its plants and gardens, but its history is even more interesting. Located in the gardens is the DuPont Garden house. This is an area devoted to the history of the Longwood Gardens and the Dupont Family. With an amazing and exciting design, it is one of the "must sees" at Longwood. With the history not only exciting but masterfully told, The DuPont House an iconic attraction at Longwood.
