Winterthur

Winterthur is one of the best places to go in the Brandywine valley to see exhibits on the arts and culture. It has a huge hall that is often filled with very unique exhibits only seen here at Winterthur. Below are some quick highlights of what Winterthur has to offer.

The Arts and Culture, All Right Here

With so many events happening at Winterthur, there is always something there that is in season. Whether its costumes from a famous TV show, or just an event to celebrate a holiday; Winterthur has everything arts related.

Beautiful spaces & Great events

Winterthur has a huge area, filled with perfectly designed events to make use of this massive space. Each event is unique, each with its own feel and style so each event is different and makes sure there is always something new to see. Each season brings new events too, Christmas you can come see the lights, or in the spring come see the musical performances by various artists. Each event is unique and perfectly designed to fit the season.

So Much To See, All In One Place

With more events being created, designed and put into action Winterthur is one of the fastest changing places I've ever seen, every time you go there, there is something new!
