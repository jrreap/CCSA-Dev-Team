Hagley Museum

With the brandywine valley being home to the DuPont company, one of the best places is all about DuPont and how the company started. If you like history, this is the place to go! Scroll down from some highlights of the museum

Take a Step Into History at Workers Hill

Take a look into lives of Irish, French, Italian, and English immigrants and how they lived and worked here at Hagley, for 120 years the workers hill was where the thousands of DuPont workers lived.

Experience Amazing Machines from the Past

See amazing peices of hitoric technology, Walk through the mist of the waterwheels, hear the rythms of the machinery in the shop, feel the blasts of blackpowder explosion demonstrations!

A Look at DuPont's legacy

In what used to be a 3 story cotton mill see what DuPonts inventions have created. You can see models and scale replicas showing what the powdermills looked like in production. Onsite there is both a racecar and a spacesuit built from Dupont materials that you can interact with!
