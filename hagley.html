Hagley Museum

With the Delaware Valley being home to the DuPont company, one of the best places is all about DuPont and how the company started. If you like history, this is the place to go! Scroll down for some highlights of the museum

Take a Step Into History at Workers Hill

Take a look into lives of Irish, French, Italian, and English immigrants and how they lived and worked here at Hagley. For 120 years the workers hill was where the thousands of DuPont workers lived and spent most of their lives during that time. It is a very important site and is a must see for anyone looking for deep history.

Experience Amazing Machines from the Past

See amazing pieces of historic technology. Walk through the mist of the waterwheels, hear the rhythms of the machinery in the shop, feel the blasts of black powder explosion demonstrations! With so many things to see and presented perfectly and seamlessly, Hagley may be one of the best attractions here in the Brandywine Valley.

A Look at DuPont's legacy

In what used to be a three story cotton mill, see what DuPont’s inventions have created. You can see models and scale replicas showing what the powder-mills looked like in production. On-site there is both a race car and a spacesuit built from DuPont materials that you can interact with! Hagley is a must see if you’re coming to the Brandywine Valley.
