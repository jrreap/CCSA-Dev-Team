Brandywine Valley

Getting to know the area rich with History, Culture and Nature.

Location

The Brandywine Valley is located on the Delmarva Peninsula, in northern Delaware. This is located perfectly covering all the main attractions of northern Delaware. Such as Longwood Gardens, Hagley Museum and Winterthur.

Weather

The weather in the area is standard weather for most middle-eastern states. The average temperature is 54 Degrees and gets about 42-45 inches of rainfall every year.

Currency

The Brandywine Valley is located in Delaware, as state of the United States of America. The currency follows the rest of the country as the dollar ($) currency.
