Longwood Gardens

Longwood is the most iconic garden in the Brandywine valley, and has many things to see. Take a look below at some iconic highlights of what Longwood has to offer.

Flowers Of Untold Beauty

Each flower is unique, everywhere you look there is a new flower design. Each flower using its color for various purposes that all come together to create one of the best gardens around.

Inspiring Events, Genius Designs

There is a new event for each season whether it is Halloween, spring or Christmas there is always something special at Longwood. With something new to see just about every day, there are more than enough reasons to head down to Longwood and check it out!

Legendary History, Masterfully Told

Longwood is known for its gardens, but its history is even more interesting. In the gardens is the DuPont Garden house where a huge area is devoted to the history of the area. With an amazing and exciting design it is one of the must sees in longwood. With the history not only exciting but masterfully told makes the DuPont house an iconic attraction at longwood.
