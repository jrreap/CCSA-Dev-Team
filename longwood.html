Longwood Gardens

Longwood is the most iconic garden in the brandywine valley, and has many things to see. Take a look below at some iconic highlights of what longwood has to offer.

The Flowers The best of the best

With so many flowers grown on site at longwood, you only really see the best of the best. Each season a new batch of flowers are grown to put in for the season. Each flower grown on site and over 24 hours are able to set up and customize an entire green house.

So Many Possiblities

There is a new event for each season wether its is Halloween, Spring or Christmas there is always something special at longwood. With something new to see just about everyday, there are more than enough reasons to head down to longwood and check it out!
