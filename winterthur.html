Winterthur

Winterthur is one of the best places to go in the Brandywine Valley to see exhibits on the arts and culture of the area. It has a huge hall that is often filled with very unique exhibits only seen here at Winterthur. Below are some quick highlights of what Winterthur has to offer.

The Arts And Culture, All Right Here

With so many events happening at Winterthur, there is always something that is in season. Whether it is a costume display from a famous television show or the famous Point to Point Steeplechase, there is always an event happening! Winterthur has everything arts related.

Beautiful Spaces and Great Events

Winterthur has a large display area filled with collections and events to make use of this massive space. Each event is unique and has its own feel and style so each event is different. With such attention to detail, there is always something new to see. Come explore the Enchanted Woods Fairy Garden in the Summer, take a Garden Tram Tour in the Fall to see the changing leaves or come to see the amazing Christmas Lights in December. Each season brings new events to Winterthur!

So Much To See, All In One Place

With more events being created, designed and put into action, Winterthur is one of the most diverse museums in the area. Every time you go, there is something new to see and do!
