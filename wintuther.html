Winterthur Museum, Garden, and Library

History

Winterthur Museum, Garden, and Library was the former home of Henry Francis du Pont. Until recently, it was known as the "Henry Francis DuPont Winterthur Museum". In the early 20th century, H. F. du Pont and his father, Henry Algernon du Pont, designed Winterthur in the spirit of 18th- and 19th-century European country houses. After he established the main building as a public museum in 1951, he moved to a smaller building on the estate.

General Info

Winterthur is a wonderful place to go if you enjoy anything arts related. With new attractions and galleries being added almost every month, Winterthur is a great place to go and play around in the gardens, or just take a look at the galleries. You can get tickets from their website located here. You can find more info about Winterthur and their events on the wevsite too.
